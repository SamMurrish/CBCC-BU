Beth Meggs
Art

Beth Meggs earned a Masters Degree in Fine Arts (MFA) from the University of North Carolina in 1995. She also studied Art at the New York Studio School in New York City, and experienced a semester of art graduate school in Cortona, Italy. Her B.S. is in Economics and Finance with a minor in fine arts from Barry University in Miami, which is where Beth was born and raised. Her Art career began as an artist assistant for Solla Clayworks in Miami. She also was an Artist in residence at Barry University, a ceramics instructor at Miami-Dade Community College, preparator at the Weatherspoon Art Gallery(UNC), and an art instructor at Rockingham Community College (printmaking,sculpture).

In addition, Beth Meggs owned and operated an art and sculpture business that provided instruction in figurative sculpture, drawing, portraiture, and printmaking. She produced life-size sculpture, murals, and commissioned pieces while marketing her own body of work at various shows and galleries. She started teaching art at Brevard Community College in 1992 and taught various studio classes. Beth now focuses on homeschooling, and teaches private lessons. She also taught a high school homeschool art class for the Brevard Heat. She is on the visual arts worship team at Church at Viera and is presently in the process of completing an extensive set of murals that encompasses the children's wing. Beth is a Christ follower, and wants the Lord to use her gifts and talents to further His kingdom.
