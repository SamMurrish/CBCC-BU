Catherine Elliott (Cathy)
English and Electives

Catherine has a BS degree in Business Management (emphasis Personnel and Government) with a History minor form Arizona State University, where she worked as a Teacher's Assistant in Speech and Writing classes. She also has a minor in Education from Florida State University. She was a substitute teacher in Brevard Public Schools for 4 years, after home schooling for six years. She taught Middle and Advanced Literature for the last 4 years at the Co-op. She directed an after school Christian club in the public schools for 11 years.

She is currently a coach and judge for Brevard County's Speech Jr./Sr. High Competition, Sr. High Debate Competition, and Jr./Sr. History Fair. She is also the coach for the Brevard County Book Bash and has been for 11 years in three schools, aiding in literature, vocabulary, and writing for 6th-8th grade, where she trains student teams to compete in Sunshine State Standards Literature competitions.

Other teaching jobs have been The Forum School for the Emotionally Disturbed, Jr/Sr. High Youth Teacher in Panama City, After -School Care Teacher, and Children's/VBS Coordinator for New Life Church in Viera and lead teacher for Faith's VBS. She has been published in: Group Magazine, Children's Magazine, Pockets, Good Housekeeping, two newspapers and an international journal. She has been a co-editor of Bay Parents Magazine and a contributing writer. Cathy has also travelled in 22 countries and has been the guest of two governments. She has a passion for getting kids interested in good books and challenging them to learn while they grow mentally and spiritually.
