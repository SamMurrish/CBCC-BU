Dan Henn
Personal Finances

Dan has a Bachelor's degree in Business and Master's degree in Accounting (Tax Specialty) from University of South Florida. He has been working in the area of tax preparation and consultation for over 20 years. He has been a CPA, Certified Public Accountant, since 1998. He worked for KPMG, a Big-four accounting firm and currently owns his own CPA firm since 2009. He is currently on the Board for Bridges, Rockledge soccer club treasurer, and Deacon at his church, Grace Fellowship.

He has been teaching Personal Finance at this co-op since 2011. He loves to educate people (his clients and students) about the myths involved with credit cards and debt. Other teaching jobs include being an adjunct professor for the University of Central Florida (Brevard campus), Florida Southern College (Orlando campus) and Valencia Community College. He currently writes a monthly article in the Florida Today for the "Ask the Business Expert" column in the Business section. He has two daughters and has been happily married to his wife, Christie, for almost twenty years.
