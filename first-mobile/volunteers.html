Thank you for volunteering at our Central Brevard Christian Co-op!
We are so grateful that you are a part of ensuring that our children have fun at Co-op, and that they are safe and respectful while doing so! Your job while on campus is to help guide students towards safe, responsible behavior that is appropriate for a learning environment as outlined in the Co-op Rules and Policies documents. Volunteers will assist with various tasks throughout the day. Young children are welcome to accompany their volunteer parent while he/she is on duty.

Signing up for your volunteer hours is fast and easy. Just follow the steps below:
  • Click the Important Paperwork Tab, then click on the Sign Up Link - this will take you to Sign Up Genius.
  • Enter your personal information (Name & Email) when prompted.
  • Pick your dates. (TWO, 3 hours shifts for first semester and TWO, 3 hours shifts for second semester.)
  • Select the option to be sent a reminder email and a link will be sent to you that you can use to reschedule, should the need arise.
Students will not be added to the class roster until your family has signed up for the required 12 volunteer hours. 6 hours should be scheduled during each semester.

For questions concerning volunteer hours, please contact Melissa Murrish at: mdmurrish@gmail.com

  • Creating Volunteer Appreciation Baskets -- One volunteer will create and fill baskets to be given to 10 lucky volunteers at our Easter Celebration Lunch 2016.
  • Dance Committee -- We need approximately 4 volunteers to organize dances and prom.
  • Hot Lunches -- We need 4-5 volunteers to provide hot crockpot lunches on a rotational schedule throughout the school year.
  • Lunch Assistants -- This will be a one semester commitment. One volunteer is needed to assist with serving lunch, and cleaning up the kitchen each semester.
  • End of Day Clean-up Crew -- This will be a one semester commitment. Two volunteers are needed each semester to assist with end of the day co-op clean-up from 2:15-3:00pm.
