Welcome to Central Brevard Christian Co-op Registration!
Registration Deadline for the 2015-2016 School Year is: June 30, 2015
(Late registration fee applies after this date.)

Classes are filled on a first come, first served basis. Families are encouraged to register early. All registration forms, and the class schedule are located on the right side of this page.

Registration for currently enrolled students will begin on April 16th 2015. Forms and administration fees may be turned in during co-op any Thursday before the end of the current school year, or at the Open House event.

Registration for NEW students will begin on April, 27th 2015 at the Open House. Forms and administration fees may be turned in during co-op any Thursday before the end of the current school year, or at the Open House event.

Co-op is pleased to announce its Open House event for the 2015-2016 school year on April, 27th 2015. The Open House event is a great opportunity to meet our tutors and discuss any questions you may have. Tutors will have their classroom curriculum on display for parents and students to view. We also, encourage you to invite other homeschool families that may be interested in Co-op to attend this event. We look forward to seeing you!
